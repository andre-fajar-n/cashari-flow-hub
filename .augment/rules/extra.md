# Extra Context: Cashari

## API Providers (Exchange Rates)
- **Free APIs**:  
  - [Exchangerate.host](https://exchangerate.host/) → free, supports crypto & commodities.  
  - [CoinGecko](https://www.coingecko.com/en/api) → free crypto data.  
  - [Metals-API](https://metals-api.com/) → gold/silver prices.  

## Naming Conventions
- Tables: snake_case.  
- Columns: snake_case.  
- Enums: lowercase string (`loan`, `borrowed`, etc.).  

## Reports & Analytics
- Cash flow reports grouped by category.  
- Net worth = wallets + investments – debts.  
- Daily balances with cumulative sums.
